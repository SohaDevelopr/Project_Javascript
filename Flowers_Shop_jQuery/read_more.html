Learn how to design flowers.

This highly popular classroom workshop is now available as an Online Workshop. Upon the successful completion of the course you will receive a Floral Design Institute Workshop Certificate.

The cost of this simple flower arrangement program for beginners is $250.00.

Want to learn how to create lovely arrangements for your home? Or, maybe you have been designing for some time and need a refresher on design theory and mechanics. Or, perhaps you would like to “test the waters” and see if a career as a floral designer is something you would like to pursue. If so, then this is the perfect workshop for you!

This simple flower arrangements workshop for beginners begins with a review of flower care and handling and working with a flower knife. You will learn about floral care products, hydration and preparation of flowers, and special conditioning requirements. You will learn how to cut flowers in a manner that is best both for the flowers and your body.

Then you will move on to designing flowers in a vase. The how-to video instruction will lead you step-by-step in the creation of a beautiful arrangement. Next is designing in floral foam. You will learn the proper methods for soaking foam and inserting flowers in foam. The step-by-step instructions lead you through the placement of foliage, main flowers, and secondary flowers so you create a dramatic design. In the final segment, you will learn to create a Hand-Tied Bouquet. This is the most challenging design, but with our step-by-step instructions, you will find that you are up to the challenge. You will be amazed at your ability to create a gorgeous Hand-Tied design.

In each simple floral arrangement lesson for beginners, there are detailed, how-to videos, written instructions, shopping lists, and online quizzes designed to help with comprehension. This is a hands-on wedding florist course workshop. You will be creating three designs, photographing, and submitting the photos to our instructors for constructive critique and valuation. Yes, this workshop is fully supported. The Floral Design Institute Tulip Team is available to answer your questions and coach you through the workshop. You will be amazed at what you learn, and the beautiful floral designs you create.

Shortly after completing your registration, the Tulip Team will be in touch with your username, password, and access to the online fundamentals florist course.
